About Me

Hello! My name is Alvaro Martinez. I am thirty-four years old and live in Whippany, New Jersey.

I currently teach people of all different ages and backgrounds how to box. Although I have a deep love for the sport and teaching, I have a craving for something more intellectually challenging. This feeling has driven me to complete my bachelors degree at Fairleigh Dickinson University and has lead me to enroll in the Rutgers coding bootcamp!



I love to learn. I love blue apron. I love dancing salsa. I love everything tech and I am super excited to enter the field.
